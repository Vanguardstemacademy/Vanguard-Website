Welcome to the Vanguard Experience

Vanguard STEM Academy is not a traditional classroom. Our programs are designed to mirror real-world engineering, consulting, technology, and professional environments. Participants work on authentic projects, collaborate with industry professionals, and are expected to demonstrate professionalism, accountability, initiative, and a commitment to excellence throughout the program.Before submitting your application, please carefully review the expectations below.

Attendance & Participation
  • Attendance is expected for all scheduled classes, workshops, meetings, presentations, and required activities.
  • Programs may include virtual sessions, in-person experiences, guest speakers, executive fireside chats, and presentations.
  • Participants are expected to be present, engaged, and on time.
  • Excessive absences, repeated tardiness, or failure to communicate may result in probation or dismissal from the program.
Academic Expectations
  • Complete all assignments by the established deadlines.
  • Participate actively in discussions, workshops, and team projects.
  • Present your work professionally.
  • Accept and apply feedback.
  • Demonstrate honesty and academic integrity in all work.
  • Participants should expect to dedicate approximately 9–11 hours per week to classes, assignments, independent learning, research, collaboration, and project development.
Professional Conduct
  • Treat classmates, instructors, mentors, partners, and guest speakers with respect.
  • Communicate professionally both online and in person.
  • Work collaboratively and value diverse perspectives.
  • Maintain a positive attitude and demonstrate leadership.
  • Follow all Vanguard policies and Code of Conduct.
Technology & Transportation
  • Participants are responsible for ensuring they have reliable internet access.
  • Access to a laptop or desktop computer capable of completing coursework.
  • Ability to install required software and access online learning platforms.
  • Reliable transportation to any required in-person sessions, presentations, site visits, or partner events.
Program Outcomes

Participants who successfully complete Vanguard programs will benefit from:

  • Building resume-ready and portfolio-ready projects.
  • Presenting their work to executives and community leaders.
  • Earning a certificate of completion.
  • Receiving mentorship from industry professionals.
  • Participating in real consulting and engineering projects.
  • Strengthening technical, leadership, and professional skills.
  • Gaining meaningful, real-world learning experiences.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to apply?

Vanguard STEM Academy offers programs for high school students, college students, working professionals, and adult learners. Eligibility varies by program and academy.

Is prior technical experience required?

No. We welcome students of all experience levels who are curious, committed, and ready to grow in a supportive environment.

What is the time commitment?

Participants typically spend 9–11 hours per week, depending on their pace and project requirements.

What happens after I submit my application?

You will receive a confirmation email. Our admissions team will review your application and contact you about the next steps.
