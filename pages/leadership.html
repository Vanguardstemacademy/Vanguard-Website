Ashley E. Delpeche

Founder & Executive Director

Ashley E. Delpeche is the Founder and Executive Director of Vanguard STEM Academy, an award-winning 501(c)(3) STEM innovation, consulting, and workforce development organization.

With training in Biomedical Engineering and Financial Engineering, her background spans research at Columbia University, big tech at Meta, and finance at Société Générale. She previously served as Zone 1 Vice Chair of the National Society of Black Engineers and was featured by Influential Women Magazine as a trailblazer.

Since founding Vanguard in 2016, Ashley has led the development of real-world engineering, education, artificial intelligence, business intelligence, and consulting experiences. Under her leadership, participants have earned official recognition from the City of Atlantic City and presented their work to global leaders.

Executives and senior leaders representing Microsoft, Google, AWS, American Express, ACT Engineers, and the NAACP praised Vanguard students’ technical abilities, professionalism, and career readiness—and stated that they would hire them. Advisors connected to the Presidential Palace of Somaliland also attended Vanguard’s graduation, and student-developed recommendations have been elevated into formal proposals for potential implementation.

Ashley’s mission is to connect talent with the skills, mentorship, visibility, and opportunities needed to create meaningful impact.
